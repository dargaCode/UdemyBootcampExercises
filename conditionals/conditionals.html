Conditional Bouncer

The bouncer will respond to your age.

  • Age 0 or lower:
    • Print an error message.
  • Age is under 18:
    • Can't enter.
  • Age is above 18 but under 21:
    • Can enter but not drink.
  • Age is 21:
    • Wish a happy birthday.
  • Age is 21 or over:
    • Can enter and drink.
  • Age is perfect square:
    • Point it out.
  • Age is odd:
    • Point it out.

Only one conditional at a time is applied.

Age will always be an integer.
